Citibikes

Summary: Citibikes Project

The purpose of this project was to observe how many people used Citibikes to observe what hours of the day from day of the week to the month of year that people usually tend to use Citibikes the most. To observe where citibikes are usually taken out from. We wanted to as well observe between gender which was the more likely to take out a Citibike as well to observe for how long they would tend to take one out.
